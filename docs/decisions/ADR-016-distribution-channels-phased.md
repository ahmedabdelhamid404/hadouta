# ADR-016: Distribution Channels — Phased A+C+E

**Status**: Accepted
**Date**: 2026-04-30
**Decision-makers**: Ahmed, Claude

## Context

Need to decide marketing channel mix for validation + launch. Egyptian-market viability filtered options (Google Search has low search volume; TV/radio too expensive; Google Ads weak). Ahmed picked A+C+E; phasing optimized for budget efficiency.

## Decision

**Three-channel mix, phased**:

- **A — Facebook + Instagram paid** (primary): Week 1 onward. 3 creatives × 3 price tiers tested first; scale winning combos.
- **C — Influencer marketing** (phased): Week 3-4 recruit nano (<10K) and micro (10K-100K) Egyptian mom influencers; their posts go live Week 5-6 with validated message; macro (100K+) saved for launch boost (Week 18+).
- **E — Organic Egyptian mom Facebook groups** (free supplement): Week 1 onward. 5-10 groups initially, expand to 15. Helpful content posts with soft Hadouta mention.

## Rationale

- Phased influencer prevents waste: don't pay influencers before message is validated
- Egyptian mom group density is unique organic asset (200K-member groups in Cairo, Alex)
- FB Ad CPM in Egypt is 5-10x cheaper than US ($1-3/1000 impressions)
- Multi-channel triangulation gives confidence in market signal
- Macro influencers reserved for launch maximizes their audience moment

## Consequences

- Marketing budget allocation: ~3K EGP Week 1-2; ~5K Week 3-4; ~7K Week 5-6; ~5K Week 7-8 = ~25-40K EGP through pre-launch
- Need UTM tracking from Day 1 to attribute signups by channel
- Influencer briefing kit must be prepared in Week 3-4 (sample books, brand assets)
- Mom group posts require helpful-content-first approach (not sales pitch) to avoid being marked spam
