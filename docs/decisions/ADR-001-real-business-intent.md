# ADR-001: Real Business Intent

**Status**: Accepted
**Date**: 2026-04-30
**Decision-makers**: Ahmed, Claude

## Context

Hadouta could be approached as: (a) a real revenue-generating business, (b) a portfolio/learning project, (c) a fast prototype. Each leads to fundamentally different scope, tech, validation, and timeline decisions.

## Decision

Hadouta is a **real business**, intended to generate meaningful side income for Ahmed and eventually become a primary revenue stream.

## Rationale

- Real business intent forces market validation **before/during** build, not after — derisks 4 months of work
- Justifies premium-tier pricing decisions (300 EGP vs commodity 50 EGP)
- Justifies investment in cultural moat (Egyptian-specific themes, settings, validators) that has long-term defensibility
- Aligns timeline pressure (Sept 2026 launch target) with real market windows (school year start)

## Consequences

- Cannot tolerate "build now, see if anyone wants it later" approach
- Validation costs (~30K EGP across 18 weeks) are mandatory budget items
- Decision-making prioritizes unit economics + scalability over learning value
- Project lives or dies on shipping by September 2026; over-engineering gets cut ruthlessly
