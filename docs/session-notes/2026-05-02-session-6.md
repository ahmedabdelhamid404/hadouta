### 2. AI-honesty correction (Ahmed's catch)

Mid-flow, Ahmed flagged that wireframe copy had drifted into "كتاب مرسوم باليد" / "الراوي بدأ يكتب" — implying human authorship. Real position is more nuanced: AI-generated content with strong Egyptian human curation + review.

Resolved as **quiet middle path** (Ahmed's framing):
- Lead with Egyptian human review + writers/illustrators behind templates + 2-3 day care window
- Don't lead with "AI generated" (premium positioning, AI = commodity in consumer minds)
- Never claim "hand-painted" / "manually written" (would feel deceived if customer learns AI is involved)
- Time as feature, not bug (2-3 days vs Hekaya's "5 minutes")
- Storyteller voice STAYS as brand archetype (the *tone*, not a claim about who writes)

Saved feedback memory `feedback_ai_honesty.md`. Brand brief amended with new section "How to talk about production publicly — the quiet middle path" (~98 lines added). Revised copy applied across all locked wireframes + design spec.
